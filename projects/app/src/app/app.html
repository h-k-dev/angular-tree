Read-only variables the tree publishes: --tree-level (per row) and --tree-row-height (the [itemSize] input). Sizing chain in this demo: --tree-toggle-size is the master control size — toggle and checkbox targets, the thread-line column, and the leaf spacer (× --tree-toggle-spacing-factor, 0.25 here) all derive from it. This demo pins it at 32px inside 40px rows; keep it ≤ the row height — a control taller than its row clips and misaligns. Full recipes: docs/THEMING.md.

Events

The tree never mutates your data. Every mutation is an intent: the tree emits, you apply it to your own state, the tree re-renders — state stays yours. Applying is your call and your timing: await a confirmation dialog and apply on confirm, or decline and simply don't — nothing to roll back (docs/RECIPES.md). All seven fire live in this playground: watch the toolbar's intent readout while you click, drag, rename, or right-click.
