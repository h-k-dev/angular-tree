Read-only variables the tree publishes: --tree-level (per row) and --tree-row-height (the [itemSize] input). Sizing chain in this demo: --tree-toggle-size is the master control size — toggle and checkbox targets, the thread-line column, and the leaf spacer (× --tree-toggle-spacing-factor, 0.25 here) all derive from it.
